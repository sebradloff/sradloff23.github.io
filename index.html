About Me

While having studied Neuroscience and pre-med in college, one introduction to computer science class sparked my interest in software engineering and changed my life's path. I realized after months of toiling with learning code at night, countless cups of coffee, and several playthroughs of Kanye West's discography later, that programming is something I loved.

In general, I love sports, most types of music, working out, escaping to the great outdoors, and being informed. I have always had the desire and motivation to utilize my utmost effort in my work, this blog being no exception.
