1) The Music Ninja: This webpage brings you to new music content in a nice layout. There are loads of graphics, layered into seeminlgy random patterns for the featured tracks section. It adds to UX with a music player on top of the page, allowing you to listen to the new music as you view content.

2) NFL.com: This webpage brings you new NFL news content. It hosts multiple forms of media, articles, photos, and videos. The layout is simplistic and relatively intuitive. A genereal league overview with specific team choices at the top of the page, followed by a menu and top news wire. The content flows down the left side of the page while the right side seems to be reserved for photos.

3) Reddit r/Fitness: This webpage brings you to the frontpage of the forum. The page is user friendly, with contenet focused on the left and forum rules, FAQ, and moderators listed on the right side.
